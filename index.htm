Keep Your Team Focused, Inspired & Accountable


Caravan is a lightweight, asynchronous daily check-in system. It's a simple replacement for a daily standup meetings that will:

1. Keep Your Team Focused & Inspired: The team leader sends out the daily update over text and/or email to the team. Some days it might be operational, others inspirational.




2. Add More Accountability With Clear Daily Communication: Everyone on the team responds with their 3 daily goals, how they're feeling, and can privately share with the team leader thier own personal development



3. Become More Reflective And Organized: Everyday Caravan sends out a mobile-friendly webpage over text or email to sync your team. As the team leader, organize and reflect on daily progress.



Caravan is especially for remote teams of 5-15 creative, smart people who want to avoid daily meetings but still keep clear daily communication.
